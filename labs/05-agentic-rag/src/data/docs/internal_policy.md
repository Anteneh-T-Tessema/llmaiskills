# Sovereign AI Internal Policy

## Overview
This document outlines the operational procedures for our Sovereign AI Content Agency. Our goal is to provide 100% accurate, research-backed content while maintaining full local control over our data and models.

## Fact-Checking Protocol
Every piece of content must be audited by the Accuracy Auditor. The auditor must cross-reference claims against the researcher's raw notes.

## Deployment Strategy
We prioritize local deployment on Apple Silicon hardware using MLX and GGUF formats. Cloud-based LLMs should only be used as fallbacks when local reasoning is insufficient.

## Groundedness Standards
Our internal threshold for groundedness is 0.9. Any content scoring below this must be rewritten.
